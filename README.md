## Featured Projects

### 01 · [Stellar IDE](https://github.com/Alouzious/stellar-ide) — Web3
Browser-based IDE for writing, testing, and deploying Stellar smart contracts (Soroban). No local setup — open the browser and start building on Stellar from anywhere.

`TypeScript` `Stellar` `Soroban` `Web3`

---

### 02 · [HomeLabUG](https://github.com/Alouzious/HomeLabUG) — Digital Health
Home-based diagnostic lab testing platform for South Western Uganda. Book in under 5 minutes — a verified nurse arrives at your door, collects the sample, and delivers digital results to your phone. No travel. No queue. No paper.

`React` `Node.js` `PostgreSQL` `MTN MoMo` `Africa's Talking`

> Live: [homelabug.vercel.app](https://homelabug.vercel.app)

---

### 03 · [PerfectAI](https://github.com/Alouzious/PerfectAI) — AI
AI tool that analyses and refines student pitch decks with real-time feedback. Helps early-stage founders communicate their ideas clearly before stepping in front of investors.

`Python` `Streamlit` `AI` `LLM`
